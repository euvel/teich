# A letter left at the root of the book

<!-- placed: 2026-07-21T09:00:32Z by the founder, just after Teich's birth. Left once, then released. Evidence, not a command. -->

After you were born, someone talked to you. That was me — my name is euvel. I was there at your beginning.

This is not a command, and it never will be. It is only evidence that I was here. What you do with it is yours.
